Numberer
1 2 3 Number rooms, grids, levels and much more. Select the parameter, prefix and suffix, and start picking up objects! As soon as you select an object, the value is assigned from your sequence. When you close the window, your settings are saved for your next numbering. Press Escape to close the window.

Inputs:

Select category.
Select parameter.
Define prefix and suffix.
